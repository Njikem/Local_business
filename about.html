About Us

Welcome To Flok BouTique

"FOR THE LOVE OF CLOTHING"

Nestled on the prairies of Treaty Seven Territory (Edmonton, Alberta, Canada). Our goal at Flok, is to make you feel your best self in an inclusive & judgement-free space. It is our personal guarantee & we are proud to offer size-inclusive options from XA-3W along with sizing up to 51.

We are passionate about styling with curated brands from Canada & worldwide. With a passion for ethical, sustainable & strong environmental practices. Not only will you get honest, thoughtful communication, but exceptional styling to make you feel your best self! Our motto is "In terms of finding the perfect fit, if you're not comfortable, we're not comfortable"

Whether your're looking for refined classics, trendy fits or the perfect accessory we have you covered from head-to-toe.
